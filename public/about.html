About

Fire safety concerns all those provisions and activities taken to avert as far as possible fire accidents, lessen the damages from such accidents, and guarantee safe escape during emergencies. Fire safety includes the general fire prevention, detection, and response strategies. Examples of factors relevant to fire safety concern the installation of systems such as smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, and sprinkler systems; good electrical wiring; and safe handling of combustible materials.

We innovate learning programs, mentorship, and industry collaborations to become bridges to the creation of a future-ready workforce. It strives to be forums for increasing employability and growth in profession along with success for all people as well as businesses.
